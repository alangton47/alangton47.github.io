Autumn!

Autumn occurs from late September to late December. During this time, school usually starts back up, and fall sports such as soccer, football, and cross country take action. In this season, the weather starts to get colder as it is a transition from summer to winter, just like spring is a transition from winter to summer. Some hollidays that occur in autumn include Haloween, Columbus Day, and Veteran's Day.

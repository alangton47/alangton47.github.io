Summer!

Summer occurs from late June to late September. During summer, the weather is usually the warmest, consisting of clear skies and hot temperatures. Additionally, most people go on holiday to warmer places in the summer due to the nice weather and school being out for summer break. Holidays that occur during summer include the Fourth of July/Independence Day, Memorial Day, and Labor Day.

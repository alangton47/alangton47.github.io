Spring!

Spring occurs from late March to late June, and takes place in between winter and summer. As seen in the photo above, spring is the time when plant life is blooming and animals are starting to appear more often. Weather during spring varies from warm sunny days to dark rainy days, as the weather is transitioning from winter to summer. Some holidays that occur in spring include Easter, Cinco De Mayo, and St. Patrick's Day.

The University of Oregon is a massive state public university located in Eugene, Oregon, consisting of around 22,000 students. The university's two primary colors are green and yellow, with their mascot being a duck. Regarding athletics, the university is well known for its football, basketball, track & field, and cross country. Additionally, a reason this school is so well known is because Phil Knight, creator of Nike, graduated from UO and now funds the university. His generous donations have led to upgrades for facilities such as the Autzen Stadium and Historic Hayward Field.

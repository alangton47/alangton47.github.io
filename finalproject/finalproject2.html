The place most people associate with Oregon, Portland has grown in size for the past two decades. Often overshadowed by Seattle, Washington, Portland has tried to step out of its shadow to become its own standalone city. When the city was first founded, it was either going to be named Portland or Boston, but ended up being named Portland due to a coin flip. Lying on the Wilamette River that feeds into to Columbia River, the city is about 25 minutes from the Washington border. Although Portland only has a population of around 1 million, the city has its own basketball team named the Portland TrailBlazers, and its own soccer team named the Portland Timbers. Surrounding downtown Portland are the suburbs Beaverton, Hillsboro, Tigard, and Gresham. While most things are in downtown Portland, a lot is located in these suburbs such as the Nike World Headquarters, Adidas USA Headquarters, Lake Oswego, Pittock Mansion, and Intel Operations.

Three Web Hosting Sites

GoDaddy

Cost

  • Regarding cost for GoDaddy, it all depends on which web-hosting plan you choose. Prices range from $8.99/mo for an "Economy" plan, to $11.99/mo for a "Deluxe" plan, to $16.99/mo for an "Ultimate" plan, and to $24.99/mo for a "Maximum" plan.
  • The more expensive plan, the greater the benefits. For example, the "Economy" plan at $8.99/mo offers only 100GB of storage, while the "Maximum" plan at $24.99/mo offers unlimited storage.

Hosting services

  • As stated in the previous section, GoDaddy has multiple plans for web-hosting, with prices varying depending on what is included in the plan.
  • "Economy" Plan at $8.99/mo - standard performance, 1 website, 100GB storage, 10 databases, unmetered bandwidth, free 1-click WordPress install.
  • "Deluxe" Plan at $11.99/mo - standard performance, unlimited websites, unlimited storage, 25 databases, unmetered bandwidth, free 1-click WordPress install.
  • "Ultimate" Plan at $16.99/mo - increased processing power, unlimited websites, unlimited storage, unlimited databases, unmetered bandwidth, free 1-click WordPress install.
  • "Maximum" Plan at $24.99/mo - increased processing and speed, unlimited websites, unlimited storage, unlimited databases, unmetered bandwidth, free 1-click WordPress install.

Domain services

  • Looking at GoDaddy's Domains page, eight domains are listed. These include .club, .net, .health, .pro, .com, .co, .global, and .org, with the most popular domain being .com. However, there is the option to search up a domain to check availability.
  • With each domain comes its own price. Disregarding the first year discount, .club is $17.99, .net is $19.99, .health is $99.99, .pro is $26.99, .com is $17.99, .co is $34.99, .global is $99.99, and .org is $20.99.

Options

  • GoDaddy offers a multitude of options for customers to choose from. On the home page of GoDaddy is a table of services that they offer. These include "domains", "website", "SSL security", "web hosting", "professional email", "WordPress", "Office 365", and "Website Design Services".
  • Additionally, within each of those categories are different plans and subcategories for customers to pick and choose. For example, "WordPress" offers four plans -similar to hosting services- to choose from depending on what you are looking for. These plans include "Basic" for $9.99, "Deluxe" for $14.99, "Ultimate" for $19.99, and "Ecommerce" for $24.99.

bluehost

Cost

  • Similar to GoDaddy, bluehost offers a multitude of plans and prices depending on what the customer is looking for. With bluehost, customers have the option to choose a plan for 12 months, 24 months, or 36 months.
  • No matter which length of time you choose, there will be four options. Looking at the 12 months list and disregarding the promotional prices, the "Basic" plan will be $9.99, the "Plus" plan will be $13.99, the "Choice Plus" plan will be $18.99, and the "Pro" plan will be $28.99.
  • Next, looking at the 24 months list and disregarding the promotional prices, the "Basic" plan will be $9.49, the "Plus" plan will be $12.99, the "Choice Plus" plan will be $17.99, and the "Pro" plan will be $27.99.
  • Finally, oberserving the 36 months list and disregarding the promotional prices, the "Basic" plan will be $8.99, the "Plus" plan will be $11.99, the "Choice Plus" plan will be $16.99, and the "Pro" plan will be $26.99.

Hosting services

  • While the prices change when the length of time is changed, the top features in each plan do not.
  • "Basic" plan - 1 website, 50 GB SSD storage, custom themes, 24/7 customer support, free domain for 1 year, free CDN included, and free SSL certificate.
  • "Plus" plan - unlimited websites, unlimited SSD storage, custom themes, 24/7 customer support, free domain for 1 year, free CDN included, free SSL certificate, and free Office 365 for 30 days.
  • "Choice Plus" plan - unlimited websites, unlimited SSD storage, custom themes, 24/7 customer support, free domain for 1 year, free CDN included, free SSL certificate, free Office 365 for 30 days, free domain privacy, and free automated backup for 1 year.
  • "Pro" plan - unlimited websites, unlimited SSD storage, custom themes, 24/7 customer support, optimized CPU resources, free domain for 1 year, free CDN included, free SSL certificate, free Office 365 for 30 days, free domain privacy, free automated backup, and free dedicated IP.

Domain services

  • While GoDaddy offered eight domains and a domain searcher, bluehost offers six domains and a domain searcher. These six domains include .com, .org, .net, .tech, .co, and .website.
  • Depending on the domain, prices will vary. On bluehost, .com is $12.99/yr, .org is $9.99/yr, .net is $14.99/yr, .tech is $4.99/yr, .co is $14.99/yr, and .website is $1.99/yr.

Options

  • Bluehost has many options for customers to choose from. These include "WordPress", "hosting", "domains", "professional services", and "email".
  • A few of these options have sub-categories to choose from. For example, "WordPress" has "WordPress hosting", "managed WordPress", and "eCommerce". "Hosting" has "shared hosting", "VPS hosting", and "dedicated hosting". Lastly, "professional services" has "services" and "blue sky".
